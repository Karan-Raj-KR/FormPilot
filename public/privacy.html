Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to FormPilot. We respect your privacy and have designed our extension architecture entirely around maximizing your personal data security. This privacy policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected.

Our guiding principle: Your data never touches our servers. Period.

1. The Information We Collect

FormPilot does not collect, store, or transmit your data to any centralized database owned by us. All data you input into the extension—including your user profiles, custom fields, and API keys—is stored locally on your own device inside your browser's local storage.

When you use the "Sign in with Google" feature, we receive basic profile information (your email address, name, and profile picture URL) directly from Google to verify your identity. This information remains encrypted within your browser.

2. Third-Party AI Integrations

To provide intelligent form-filling capabilities, the text on the webpages you scan and the personal profile data you have selected are transmitted securely and directly to the AI provider you have chosen to configure (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini).

  • This communication occurs directly between your browser and the respective AI provider's API servers.
  • Your private API key is used strictly to authenticate these requests and is never visible to us.
  • We strongly recommend reviewing the specific privacy policies of your chosen AI provider to understand how they handle data transmitted through their APIs.

3. Data Sharing

Because we do not have access to your data, we cannot, do not, and will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone.

4. Security

We use industry-standard browser storage security to protect your data. Your API keys are retained exclusively inside chrome.storage.local, isolated natively by the Chrome browser runtime environment.

5. Your Rights

Because you retain absolute physical control over your data, you can exercise your right to erasure at any time simply by uninstalling the extension or clearing the extension's local data.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the security practices of FormPilot, please reach out via our GitHub repository issues page.
